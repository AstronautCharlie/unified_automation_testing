⏺ Here are the estimated locomotion durations per subject across all 5 ADL
  recordings, derived by classifying posture using the back IMU (gravity
  orientation) and walking using shoe angular velocity:

  ---
  Method:
  - Posture (standing vs. sitting vs. lying): inferred from the direction of the
   static gravity vector in the back IMU accelerometer — upright when the
  dominant axis alignment is strong, lying when it's nearly horizontal, sitting
  in between.
  - Walking vs. Standing: within upright postures, walking is detected when the
  rolling standard deviation of shoe angular velocity exceeds a threshold
  (capturing the foot rotation patterns of walking steps).
